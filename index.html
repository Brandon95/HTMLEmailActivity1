The Seagulls won every single game for fifty years, leaving the BeanTown Bombers 0-50. Could the Bombers suprise the Seagulls this year and win their first game of the championship series?

On July 4th, 2015, for 2 hours at 1:00PM, two of the most popular cities in the world will go head to head in a baseball game, and the winner will be declared the City Series champion.

Burgers and hotdogs will be available along with ice cream and cotton candy all for just two dollars!
